About Us

"Picking The Tees is organised by RiverTees Rediscovered. It is designed to be a platform that promotes and support local community action. If you would like to get in touch with RiverTees Rediscovered, then please follow this link to our website or to our contact page.

Objective of Picking The Tees

Simplicity at its best: RiverTees Rediscovered are committed to provide the best experience for our volunteers to manage their litterpicking projects. Picking the Tees will be a Web Platform for residents of the River Tees area to propose areas where a litter picking project is required and also submit their litterpicking projects here. All the updates regarding litterpicking projects involving RiverTees Rediscovered will be displayed here.
